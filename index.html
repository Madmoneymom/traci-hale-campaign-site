Traci D. Hale

Candidate for Prichard, Alabama City Council – District 3

About Me

I'm Traci D. Hale — a mom, a neighbor, a friend, and a lifelong advocate for our community. I believe leadership starts at home and grows from genuine connection. That’s why I bring the heart of a neighbor and the mind of a leader to the table.

With a double MBA in Public Administration and Business Management, I understand both the systems that govern our city and the strategies that help it thrive. I’m running for Prichard City Council – District 3 to bring smart planning, greater transparency, and lasting progress to the place we call home.

My commitment is to put people first — to listen, to lead with integrity, and to fight for safer streets, stronger local businesses, and services that truly serve all of District 3. I’m not here to talk at you — I’m here to work with you.
